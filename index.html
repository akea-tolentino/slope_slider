Welcome to Slope Slider. Are you ready to hit the slopes?
Careful! There are obstacles in the way!
Avoid them by using the: "A" key to move left,
"D" key to move right, or the "S" key to jump over them.
Be warned, a jump has to be timed right in order to succeed.
Your score will be calculated based on how many successful jumps you've completed and how long the ride was.
When you're ready to start, hit the play button above.
See you at the end of your ride!
